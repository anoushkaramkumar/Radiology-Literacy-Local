Welcome to the Radiology Literacy Initiative!


We are the very first AI-powered Radiology Literacy Initiative tool. Our website is meant to be a proof of concept, dedicated to improving health literacy by providing accessible and easy-to-understand health information to patients. We understand that medical jargon can be confusing and intimidating. We believe that everyone should have access to clear and reliable health information, so they can make more informed health decisions. We hope this resource becomes an invaluable resource for patients, families, hospitals, and providers.

Thank you for choosing our website as one of your sources for radiology information. We are committed to providing high-quality, accessible, and engaging health information for patients.

This project was created by Rushabh Doshi, a medical student at Yale, Kanhai Amin, a Yale undergraduate student, and Pavan Khosla, a Yale medical student.
